Deforestation and its Causes

What is deforestation?

A forest is a large area covered with trees and plants. It provides habitat for animal and plants. The process of clearing away forests by cutting down and burning them is known as deforestation. People use the wood or the perishable natural resources to make way for farmingand development.

Causes of Deforestation

Procuring land for cultivation
Forest trees are cut down to obtain more agricultural land for cultivation of crops to feed the increasing population of our country.

Building houses and factories
The trees are cut down to build homes, factories, roads, dams, etc.

Making various products
These such as paper or wood for making doors, windows, etc., are obtained from the trees.

Natural Causes
It includes forest fires, severe droughts, etc.
